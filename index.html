What we treat

Our Specialities

Evidence-based physiotherapy across every speciality — from sports injuries to paediatric conditions.

Sports Rehabilitation

Comprehensive recovery from acute and chronic sports injuries including muscle strains, ligament tears, tendinopathies, and joint instabilities. Designed for athletes at every level.

  • ACL & MCL rehabilitation
  • Rotator cuff injuries
  • Ankle sprains & fracture rehab
  • Return-to-sport programmes

Post-Surgery Recovery

Structured post-operative rehabilitation protocols for orthopaedic, spinal, and joint replacement surgeries. We work in coordination with your surgeon to ensure optimal outcomes.

  • Knee & hip replacement rehab
  • Spinal fusion recovery
  • ORIF and fracture fixation
  • Shoulder reconstruction

Back & Spine Therapy

Evidence-based treatment for all forms of back pain, disc conditions, sciatica, and spinal stenosis. We address the root cause, not just the symptoms.

  • Disc herniation (L4-L5, L5-S1)
  • Sciatica & radiculopathy
  • Spondylosis & spondylolisthesis
  • Postural correction

Neurological Physio

Specialised rehabilitation for patients with neurological conditions affecting movement, balance, and coordination. We use task-oriented training and neuroplasticity principles.

  • Stroke & hemiplegia recovery
  • Parkinson's disease management
  • Multiple sclerosis physiotherapy
  • Peripheral neuropathy

Paediatric Physio

Gentle, child-friendly physiotherapy for infants, toddlers, and children. We make therapy engaging and effective using play-based approaches.

  • Developmental delay
  • Cerebral palsy management
  • Torticollis & posture
  • Sports injuries in children

Geriatric Physio

Comprehensive physiotherapy for elderly patients focused on maintaining independence, preventing falls, and managing age-related musculoskeletal conditions.

  • Fall prevention programmes
  • Osteoporosis management
  • Balance & coordination training
  • Arthritis pain management

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

What conditions do you treat at OT Nest?
We treat a wide range of musculoskeletal, neurological, and sports-related conditions including back pain, neck pain, arthritis, sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, stroke rehabilitation, Parkinson's disease, and paediatric developmental conditions.
Do I need a doctor's referral to book an appointment?
No referral is required. You can book directly through our website, call us, or walk in. However, if you have recent reports, X-rays, or MRI scans, please bring them along — they help us plan your treatment more accurately.
How long is each physiotherapy session?
A standard session is approximately 45 minutes. Initial assessments may take up to 60 minutes. The exact duration depends on your condition and treatment requirements.
How many sessions will I need?
This varies widely based on your condition, severity, and overall health. Acute injuries may require 4–8 sessions, while chronic or post-surgical conditions may need 12–20 sessions. Dr. Satish will give you a personalised estimate after your initial assessment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking through Razorpay. You can also pay online when booking through our website. Most major health insurance panels are accepted.
Do you offer home visit physiotherapy?
Yes, we offer home visits for patients who are unable to travel due to their condition. Home visit charges are ₹1,200 per session. Please contact us on WhatsApp or call to arrange a home visit.
What is your cancellation policy?
We request at least 4 hours' notice for cancellations. Appointments cancelled with less than 4 hours' notice may incur a ₹100 fee. Emergency cancellations are handled case by case.
Do you have clinics in both Patna and Gurgaon?
Yes! Our Gurgaon clinic is at H No. 1042, Block G, Sector 57 and our Patna clinic is at D-12, Boring Road, Near PMCH. Both clinics are fully equipped and operate Monday to Saturday, 8am to 8pm.
