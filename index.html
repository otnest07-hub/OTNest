Meet the doctor

Dr. Satish

MPT (Orthopaedics) · OT Nest Founder

Dr. Satish is a passionate and highly skilled physiotherapist specialising in orthopaedic and sports rehabilitation. With over five years of dedicated practice and more than 1,300 patients treated, he has established OT Nest as a trusted name in physiotherapy across Gurgaon and Patna.

His evidence-based approach combines manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and advanced modalities including dry needling and ultrasound therapy. Every treatment plan is personalised — never generic — built around each patient's specific goals, lifestyle, and recovery timeline.
